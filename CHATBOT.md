# Chatbot System Documentation
# InvestSense AI

---

# 1. Overview

The InvestSense AI Chatbot is an AI-powered investment analysis assistant designed to help beginner investors understand stock-related information through conversational interaction.

The chatbot focuses on:
- sentiment analysis
- technical explanations
- market understanding
- risk awareness
- rational investment guidance

The chatbot is NOT:
- a trading bot
- customer support
- a brokerage assistant
- a stock execution platform

---

# 2. Main Objectives

The chatbot aims to:
- simplify investment analysis
- explain financial concepts clearly
- provide beginner-friendly insights
- reduce FOMO-driven investing behavior
- support rational decision-making

---

# 3. Core Features

# 3.1 Stock Analysis Assistance

Users can:
- ask about stock sentiment
- request market summaries
- ask for volatility explanations
- request technical indicator insights
- ask beginner-focused investment questions

---

# 3.2 Technical Indicator Explanation

Supported indicators:
- RSI
- MACD
- Trend analysis
- Volatility indicators

The chatbot explains indicators using:
- simplified language
- educational interpretation
- beginner-friendly context

---

# 3.3 Sentiment Analysis

The chatbot provides:
- Positive sentiment
- Neutral sentiment
- Negative sentiment

based on:
- financial news
- market-related text data
- analyzed market information

---

# 3.4 Risk Awareness Guidance

The chatbot may provide:
- volatility warnings
- market caution indicators
- anti-FOMO reminders
- rational investment prompts

Example:
- “Market conditions remain volatile.”
- “Negative sentiment may increase uncertainty.”
- “Consider evaluating market risks before making decisions.”

# 9. Anti-FOMO Guidance

The chatbot includes behavioral-awareness guidance mechanisms.

The AI may:
- remind users about market risk
- explain uncertainty
- encourage rational evaluation
- discourage impulsive investing decisions

Goal:
Encourage logical investment thinking rather than emotional reactions.

---

# 10. Beginner-Friendly Experience

The chatbot is designed for users with limited financial knowledge.

The AI prioritizes:
- simplified explanations
- understandable terminology
- educational responses
- contextual learning

---

# 11. Design Direction

The chatbot interface should feel like:
- a modern AI investment workspace
- a financial intelligence assistant
- an analytical AI platform

Avoid:
- Discord-like interfaces
- gaming UI
- generic chatbot appearance
- crypto exchange aesthetics

---

# 12. Platform Limitations

The chatbot does NOT:
- provide guaranteed financial advice
- execute stock transactions
- connect to brokerage systems
- automate trading decisions

The chatbot functions solely as:
- an educational assistant
- an analytical AI tool
- a decision-support system
